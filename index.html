The Shakespeare Awards

The Best Romeo and Juliet Character

Nominees

  • Romeo
  • Juliet
  • Mercutio
  • County Paris
  • Benvolio

And the Winner is...

Mercutio

Fun-loving and witty, with humour that can sometimes be facetious and course, Mercutio is a close friend to Romeo.

~

The Best Macbeth Character

Nominees

  • Macbeth
  • Macduff
  • Banquo
  • Three Witches
  • Lady Macbeth

And the Winner is...

Banquo

At first an ally to Macbeth, but as the play progresses he goes on to play many different roles, one of which is a foil to Macbeth, resisting evil while Macbeth embraces it.

~

The Best Merchant of Venice Character

Nominees

  • Jessica
  • Shylock
  • Antonio
  • Launcelot Gabbo
  • Gratiano

And the Winner is...

Jessica

In the play, Jessica is minor but pivitol role. Historically, her character has often been view negatively, but recently a new, more positive, light has been shed on her character.

~
